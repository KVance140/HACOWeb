About Us –––– 
It's all about people

Our Team

Haurs & Co. Transportation was started in 2942 with a dream by the founders of running the most reliable and affordable transportation empire in the universe. They wanted more out of current transportation companies, as all of them had their own individual flaws that did a disservice to their customers, which the founders felt they could rectify. Some were affordable, yes, but did not have the comfort their customers deserved; others were extremely expensive, but did not provide the service warranted by the price. Thus, affordability and comfort were at the forefront of the founders' vision. Reliability was simply a byproduct of both of these factors that needed to be worked on every day.

Our Mission

The mission of Haurs & Co Transportation has always been to enable affordable, reliable, comfortable, and world-class transportation to all citizens of the United Empire of Earth.

We hire experienced pilots, security personnel, flight attendants, and others to provide the service we believe everyone deserves.

Our Charter

We are authorized, through the Board of Transportation within the United Empire of Earth (UEE), to conduct passenger and cargo transportation flights in cooperation with all local, regional, and governmental regulations throughout all UEE territories and systems. We will comply with all local, regional, and governmental orders through officers or officials of the UEE.
